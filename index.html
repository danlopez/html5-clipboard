About noteThis

noteThis is a way to quickly and easily take notes online. If you use it on one computer, it will use your local storage to save your notes for the next time you come back. If you connect your Facebook or Twitter account, your notes will be saved in the cloud and accessible from any computer that you sign in with. The icon next to your note tells you if a note is a cloud note or a local note:

= Cloud note, saved online
= Local note, saved on your computer only

noteThis was built by Defined Clarity to use internally. We wanted a superfast way to jot down notes in our browser. Other note taking apps took a long time to load and seemed overburdened with features, so we built our own. We hope you enjoy using it as much as we do!

The backed is powered by the good folks at Firebase. Their real-time backend saves your notes for you and makes sure that you get them wherever you are!
